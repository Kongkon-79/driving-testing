OAuth Google Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25.04.2025

Driving Test Route Assistant (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application available in the United Kingdom.

This policy is in compliance with Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements, and applies to all users of the Driving Test Route Assistant app.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Email Address: Required during registration and login via Google OAuth to create and manage your account.

b. Location Data

  • Real-Time Location: Used to display and guide you along nearby driving test routes.
  • Location data is only accessed while the app is in use and is not shared with third parties.

c. Purchase Data

  • Google Play Purchase Information: We collect data related to your in-app purchases through the Google Developer Platform to manage subscriptions and offer premium services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your identity through your Google account.
  • Provide personalized, real-time driving test route suggestions.
  • Enable location-based route tracking and guidance.
  • Process and manage in-app subscription payments.
  • Improve our services and enhance user experience.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data is not shared with external services except as required for:

  • Google OAuth login and account authentication.
  • Payment processing via Google Play.
  • Legal obligations or to comply with applicable laws.

4. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These include secure storage, encryption where appropriate, and access controls.

5. Google User Data Policy Compliance

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only use your data to provide or improve the features of the app and do not transfer it to others except to provide or improve our app functionality.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Revoke access to your Google account via your Google Account settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • Disable location access at any time via your device settings.

Note: Disabling location access may affect your ability to use key features of the app.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you within the app or via email.
